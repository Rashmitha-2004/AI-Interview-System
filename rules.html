Interview Exam Instructions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must have a degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Minimum academic score of 70% throughout education.
  • Experience with programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++ is preferred.
  • Must be available to work full-time and relocate if necessary.

Exam Rules

  • Use of any external resources (books, websites, or notes) is strictly prohibited.
  • Ensure your internet connection is stable throughout the exam.
  • Each question must be answered within the given time frame. No pausing allowed.
  • Be honest and complete all sections of the exam independently.
  • Any form of cheating or misconduct will lead to disqualification.

Timing Details

The interview exam is divided into three sections, each with specific time allocations:

  • Section 1: 30 minutes for coding tasks.
  • Section 2: 20 minutes for behavioral questions.
  • Section 3: 15 minutes for problem-solving and logic questions.

The total exam duration is 65 minutes. Please ensure you are available for the entire duration.
