Aptitude Quiz

1. What is the next number in the sequence: 2, 4, 8, 16, ...?

2. If 5x - 3 = 2x + 12, what is the value of x?

3. What is the value of (7 × 3) + (5 × 2)?

4. If the price of 5 books is $60, what is the price of 8 books?

5. A train travels 150 miles in 3 hours. What is its average speed?

6. If 3 pencils cost $1.50, how much do 7 pencils cost?
